Corrigan's Cycling Club

Who We Are


We are a family-run cycling club operating in the picturesque town of Westport in Co Mayo. After many combined years involved in Club racing, Youth racing, Leisure cycling and Mountain biking, we wanted to give back to the community and share our love of cycling with the local youth.


We meet at the Skate Park on the West Road. With lots of space, free from any traffic, it's an ideal area for teaching beginners. In a seperate section, there's ramps and obsticles for the more advanced cyclists. From here we we can take advantage of the Greenway path, perfect for cycling, leading us to the grounds of Westport House to enjoy a picnic on our "Fun Family Days"!
